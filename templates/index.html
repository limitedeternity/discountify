About

This project was created to share discounting cards with other people.
You may ask "Why should I do that?".
Well, some discounting cards have a loyality system. The more you buy, the more discount becomes. By sharing card with other people you can maximize discount for your card really fast. And you are not loosing anything at all!
Also, people, who use your discounting card will get, obviously, a discount.
Everyone love discounts, isn't it?
